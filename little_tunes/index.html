Little Tunes is a popular group class for children ages 3 to 6 years old that prepares students for private lessons, encourages music appreciation, and develops organization and study skills.

Activities in Little Tunes classes are dynamic and physical, teaching the basics of music –– rhythm, pitch and harmony –– through a variety of games and interactive activities.

Ideal for young, developing musicians, Little Tunes activities help develop hand/eye coordination, finger independence and classroom etiquette.
